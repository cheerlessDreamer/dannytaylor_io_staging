Cytiva formerly GE Healthcare

Product Designer, August 2020 – June 2022

Context

Cytiva needed to turn complex lab equipment and biopharma workflows into intuitive, configurable digital experiences. The existing tools were fragmented and difficult for scientists to use effectively.

Role

I led product design across multiple platforms, working closely with product managers and engineers to define UX patterns and visual systems that could scale across Cytiva’s suite of instruments and software.

What we built

Outcome

Shipped unified design patterns and interfaces across Oligosynt, Unicorn, and related platforms, improving consistency and usability for lab personnel. Ongoing iteration based on customer feedback.
